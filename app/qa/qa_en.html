Book Purchase Related

  1. 1. In what ways may I purchase e-books？

    2. （1）Inspirata eBooks platform: ebook.endao.co

    （2）Android App supports in-app purchase, please click here to download

    Note: Inspirata eBooks App installed through Apple App Store does not support book purchase at the moment.

  2. 2. What payment options are available to me when purchasing books？

    3. Inspirata eBooks supports Alipay and credit card payment, and Alipay payment is currently only available for users in mainland China.

  3. 3. Can I request a return/refund for a purchased e-book？

    4. Unfortunately, you are purchasing a digital reading product and cannot apply for a refund or exchange after a successful purchase.

  4. 4. How to read purchased e-books？Can I read them through a Kindle reader?

    5. 4.1. You can download the Inspirata eBooks App on iOS, Android, PC and Mac to read e-books, click here for details.

    The PC/Mac reader currently only supports basic reading and listening functions, and other features will be added gradually.

    4.2. Kindle users can only read e-books purchased on the Amazon platform or e-books that are not protected by DRM copyright. Please understand that our e-books are protected by DRM copyright and cannot be read on Kindle.

  5. 5. What type of book am I purchasing？

    6. You are purchasing an EPUB formatted, DRM protected e-book from Inspirata eBooks.

  6. 6. Can I buy an e-book as a gift to a friend？

    7. Yes. Click the "Gift" button on the “book details” page to purchase the book as a gift to someone else. After successful payment, you can fill in the email address of the recipient in "Me > My gift history”. The other party can redeem the book in their "Me > Use redeem code" after receiving the book redemption code.


Library Management Related

  1. 1. I have already purchased e-books, why does the library show empty after I sign in to the App？

    2. Your purchased e-books will be displayed in "Me > Books" by default. If you want to add some or all of your purchased books to your library, please go to the "Me > Books" page and complete this operation through the "Download" button.

  2. 2. How can I manage the e-books in the library?

    3. Long click on the cover of the e-books or click on the "Edit" icon at the top right corner of the library to enter the management mode, where you can remove or group e-books in batches. "Remove download" will delete the local files of the corresponding e-books, but will not delete the notes/underlines you have made.

  3. 3. How do I continue to read a book after I have removed a purchased e-book from the library?

    4. The e-books removed from the library will still appear in "Me > Books". You just need to go to this page and download the book again.

  4. 4. I am updating e-books in the App. Why does it say "update failed"?

    5. Usually, "update failed" is caused by the network. Please check your network connection or try again later.

  5. 5. Can I sign in to the App and read purchased e-books on multiple devices with the same Inspirata account?

    6. Yes. The same Inspirata account can be signed in a maximum of 5 devices at the same time, but the sixth device will not be able to sign in successfully. This means that you can read purchased e-books on up to 5 devices. You can manage your bundled devices through "Me > My devices" on the website.

  6. 6. When I read an e-book in one device, can my reading/devotional progress, grouping information, notes, underlines, and bookmarks be synchronized to other devices?

    7. Yes. You only need to use the same Inspirata account to sign in to the App, and the above data will be automatically synchronized and updated when the network connection is normal. However, in the new device, you still need to manually download these e-books to the library.


Book Reading Related

  1. 1. Can I read the e-book I have purchased offline?

    2. Yes. Simply download it to your library and the e-book data will be saved locally. However, if the e-book is updated when you read the book offline, the update cannot be downloaded.

  2. 2. How can I view the paperback page numbers in the e-book?

    3. Click on the "AA" icon in the top right corner of the reading interface, and turn on "Page numbers" at the bottom of the pop-up menu bar. The orange tabs that appear in the reading interface are the paper book page numbers (for example, the content between tabs 7 and 8 belongs to page 7)

  3. 3. How to get help if I have other problems？

Devotional Functions Related

  1. 1. How to use devotional functions？

    2. You can select the devotional/bible reading materials you are interested in from the App "Devotional > All Devotionals". Click "Subscribe" to receive daily devotions/readings online in "My Devotionals", write notes on your feelings and punch in when you are done.

  2. 2. Why can I only read the devotional plan for the day？

    3. If you have not purchased the book, you will only be able to view the day's devotions and will not be able to catch up on the reading or preview other content. After purchasing the book, you can read all the contents and jump to the unpunched date through the calendar function to "catch up".

  3. 3. Can I export my devotional notes?

    4. You can go to "Me> Notes>Devotionals" in the App to enter the details of the notes corresponding to the spirituality material. Click the "Export" button in the upper right corner to export your devotional notes in bulk.
